FAQ patients ask before they call

Can I request an appointment without calling?

Yes. Use the Book form anytime — including nights and weekends. We confirm by phone/email.

Do you accept public insurance?

Yes for many treatments. Share your insurer when booking so we can guide expectations.

I’m anxious about dental visits — what helps?

We explain every step before treatment, offer breaks, and keep a calm room environment.

How fast can I be seen for pain?

Tell us it’s urgent in the form. We prioritize pain cases when slots open.

Is this website only marketing?

No. It’s built to solve three practice problems: get found, get trusted, and get booked.
