Market research

Why we start with dental / medspa

Best first niche

Dental & clinical aesthetics

High patient LTV, already spend on marketing, treat website as patient-acquisition asset not a brochure. Typical professional dental sites cost far more than $1,499.

Runner-up

Medspa / aesthetics

Premium brand sensitivity + high ticket treatments. Strong willingness to pay for a polished site that converts consults.

Later

Restaurants / gyms

Need websites, but often tighter budgets and slower decision cycles for a $1,499 one-time package.

What a dental website must do

  • Attract: local SEO / service pages for searches like dentist + city.
  • Convert: Book CTA, reviews, team, before/after, insurance clarity.
  • Capture after hours: online appointment request when phone is closed.
  • Reduce anxiety: real clinic photos, credentials, plain-language process.

Evidence snapshot (public market data)

  • Local service providers (dentists, clinics, consultants) are repeatedly listed among businesses that most need websites for credibility + local clients.
  • Dental website projects commonly price in the thousands ($3.5k–$9k+ range for quality builds) — our $1,499 sits as an easy “done-for-you preview → ownership” wedge.
  • Dental/medical site conversion averages around ~4%+ when the site is solid; practices already buy ads/SEO, so a better site multiplies existing spend.
  • Medspa custom sites are often positioned as premium growth assets ($7.5k–$30k narratives in industry guides) — again, willingness to pay is real.

Sources informing this pick: HostAdvice “businesses that need websites”, dental website cost/conversion guides (PatientGain, Rosemont), medspa website pricing guides, local-service website demand writeups.

Market pivot

Skip hard markets. Sell where USDT feels normal.

Germany can work later, but local SMBs are often conservative on crypto payments. For first revenue, prioritize cities where founders already use USDT / digital assets.

Primary

Dubai / UAE

Crypto hub, English works for clinics/medspas, high willingness to pay for premium websites, founders comfortable with USDT settlement.

Secondary

Istanbul

Strong retail crypto culture + dental tourism clinics. English/Turkish outreach. Competitive but high volume of weak clinic sites.

Also good

Lisbon · Miami · Singapore

Crypto-friendly lifestyle hubs / English markets. Use after Dubai pipeline is running.

Why not Germany first?

Not impossible — just slower for a USDT-only checkout. Local patients want German sites; many practice owners are process-heavy and bank-first. For sale #1, optimize for speed-to-cash: English + crypto-comfortable decision makers.
